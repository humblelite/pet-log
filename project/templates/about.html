About

Virtual pet logger is an application that allows pet business and owners to keep a log of all their pets. Once logged in each user has a dashboard where the will be able to create a record of their pets type (dog, cat, bird, fish, critter, reptile), name, and description. Virtual pet logger is a very useful application for those who have multiple pets and want to have some way to organize them.
